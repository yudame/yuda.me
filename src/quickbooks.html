Frequently Asked Questions

Is my QuickBooks data secure?

Yes! We use OAuth 2.0 for authentication and never store your QuickBooks credentials. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. The MCP runs locally on your machine.

Which versions of QuickBooks are supported?

Currently, we support QuickBooks Online. QuickBooks Desktop support is coming soon.

Do I need Claude Desktop?

Yes, QuickBooks MCP is designed to work with Claude Desktop. You'll need an active Claude subscription to use the integration.

Can I undo actions performed by the AI?

Yes! All actions are logged and can be reviewed. QuickBooks' native audit trail captures all changes, and you can revert any modifications if needed.

What kind of support do you offer?

We offer email support for all plans, with priority support for Professional users and dedicated support for Enterprise customers.
